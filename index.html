Frequently Asked Questions

What is a SAR / DSAR?

A subject access request is a request from an individual — for schools, usually a parent on behalf of their child, or a staff member — for a copy of the personal data you hold about them, made under UK GDPR. Schools normally have one calendar month to respond.

Can you handle safeguarding records?

Yes — safeguarding-heavy requests are our core work. We prepare the material with each third party considered individually, flag anything that needs a judgement call, and leave final disclosure decisions with your DSL, DPO or head, which is where they belong.

How quickly can you turn a request around?

It depends on volume and complexity, but we plan every job around your statutory deadline and confirm a delivery date when we quote. Urgent requests can often be prioritised.

How do you keep our data secure?

Files are transferred and stored securely, access is restricted to those working on your request, and all material is deleted after completion in line with our retention policy. We're happy to sign an NDA or data processing agreement.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. VeriRequest provides practical, operational SAR support — redaction, triage, and drafting. Final decisions on disclosure and any legal questions remain with your school and its advisers or DPO.

How is pricing calculated?

We review the scope of your request — the volume of material, its complexity, and the deadline — and give you a single fixed price before any work begins. There is no hourly billing.

What do you need from us to get started?

A short description of the request and, ideally, an indication of the volume of material involved. We'll confirm scope and arrange secure file transfer from there.
