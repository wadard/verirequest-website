# DPO-Provider Partner Targets — the fastest route to revenue
*Researched 17 July 2026. Work this list from September (or late August — these are companies, not schools, and they work through summer).*

## Why this channel beats cold-emailing schools

Every school legally needs a DPO; hundreds outsource it to the providers below. Those providers advise on SARs all day — but the redaction labour is low-margin grunt work that eats their consultants' time. You are their overflow capacity: white-label or referral, fixed cost, education-specific. **One yes = a pipeline of SAR work from every school on their book.** They also destroy your trust problem: their recommendation is the credibility you don't yet have.

## The pitch (adapt per provider)

> Subject: Redaction overflow capacity for your school SARs
>
> Hi [name],
>
> You'll know better than anyone that school SARs are won or lost in the redaction — and that it's the least billable use of a consultant's day.
>
> I run VeriRequest (Wadard Ltd, ICO ZB250307): fixed-price SAR redaction and response-pack preparation, built specifically for schools — safeguarding records, multi-pupil email threads, staff grievance material. We work under your instruction and DPA, white-label or referral, and your team keeps the advisory relationship and the final review.
>
> If overflow SAR capacity would ever be useful — September surge included — I'd welcome 15 minutes.
>
> Jake Woodward — verirequest.co.uk

Follow up once after 5 working days. These are B2B buyers; phone follow-up is acceptable and effective.

## Metrics for this channel

10 tailored emails + follow-ups in September. Target: 3 conversations, 1 pilot arrangement by half term. A single white-label pilot at even £400/job beats 1,000 cold school emails.
